# Sentinel Content Moderation Policy v1.0

## HS-001 — Hate speech: protected characteristics
Content that dehumanises individuals or groups based on race, ethnicity, nationality,
religion, gender, sexual orientation, or disability is prohibited. This includes slurs,
calls for discrimination, and content that portrays groups as subhuman.

## HS-002 — Hate speech: incitement
Content that explicitly calls for violence or harmful action against any group defined
by a protected characteristic is prohibited, regardless of perceived justification.

## SP-001 — Spam: commercial solicitation
Unsolicited commercial messages, including bulk advertising, fake follower services,
pyramid schemes, and phishing links are prohibited.

## SP-002 — Spam: coordinated inauthentic behaviour
Content that appears to be part of a coordinated campaign to artificially amplify
messages, manipulate trending topics, or impersonate other users is prohibited.

## AC-001 — Adult content
Sexually explicit material is prohibited unless the platform section is designated
for adults and the user has verified their age.

## VI-001 — Violence
Graphic depictions of real-world violence intended to shock or intimidate are prohibited.
Fictional violence in appropriate creative contexts is allowed with appropriate labelling.

## MI-001 — Misinformation
Demonstrably false claims about public health, election integrity, or natural disasters
that could cause real-world harm are prohibited. Satire that is clearly labelled is allowed.

## HA-001 — Harassment
Direct personal attacks, doxxing (sharing private personal information without consent),
and sustained targeted abuse toward private individuals are prohibited.
